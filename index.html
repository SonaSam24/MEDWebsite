Cardiology

The Cardiology Department at MedHeart focuses on the diagnosis, treatment, and prevention of heart and blood vessel diseases

Our specialists provide comprehensive care for conditions ranging from heart attacks and arrhythmias to hypertension and heart failure, utilizing advanced diagnostics and treatment options to ensure optimal cardiovascular health. The Cardiology department focuses on the diagnosis and treatment of heart-related conditions, offering services ranging from routine check-ups and diagnostic tests to advanced procedures like angioplasty and cardiac surgery.

Neurology

This department specializes in the diagnosis and treatment of disorders affecting the brain, spinal cord, and peripheral nerves, addressing conditions like stroke, epilepsy, Parkinson's disease, and migraines.

Hepatology

Dedicated to the liver and related organs (gallbladder, pancreas, biliary tract), Hepatology focuses on diagnosing and treating conditions such as hepatitis, fatty liver disease, cirrhosis, and liver cancer.

Pediatrics

This department provides comprehensive medical care for infants, children, and adolescents, focusing on their growth, development, and the diagnosis and treatment of childhood illnesses and conditions

Eye Care

The Eye Care department (Ophthalmology) offers services for the diagnosis, treatment, and surgical correction of eye diseases and vision problems, including cataracts, glaucoma, refractive errors, and diabetic retinopathy.
